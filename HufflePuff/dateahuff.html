Top 5 Reasons to Date a Hufflepuff

So you're considering dating a Hufflepuff, eh? Great choice. Not only are Hufflepuffs amongst the least evil of all Wizards, they also make great life-long companions. Here's a quick list of the top five reasons you should date a Puff.

#5 - They're down to Earth

You won't often find a Hufflepuff bragging about their most recent exploits, and not because they have so few. Hufflepuffs are naturally humble and when they brag it is in celebration with friends.

#4 - They're Loyal

Much like the badger that dawns their crest, Hufflepuffs will do anything to protect their own. They are loyal almost to a fault, but don't let that turn you off!

#3 - They're down for anything

From battling evil wizards to bowling, from pulling screaming baby plants out of the ground to rock climbing, Hufflepuffs are down for it all.

#2 - They're sometimes great botanists

Speaking of plants, many Hufflepuffs offer a green thumb and can usually put that to good use as a chef.
